Analysis of Mi Último Adiós

Guide Questions for the Analysis

1. Reconciliation vs. Defiance

How does Rizal navigate the themes of reconciliation with the Spanish government and defiance in the face of oppression within Mi Último Adiós? Analyze the poem for instances where he might express both and explore the reasons behind this.

2. Legacy and Sacrifice

What image of himself does Rizal want to leave behind for the Filipino people? Examine how he portrays his sacrifice and how it connects to the future of the Philippines.

3. Patriotism and Love

How does Rizal define patriotism and love for one's country in Mi Último Adiós? Explore the language he uses and the imagery associated with these concepts.
